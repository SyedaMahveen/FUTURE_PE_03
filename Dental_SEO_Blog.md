# Best Dental Care Tips for Healthy Teeth

## Why Dental Care Matters

Maintaining oral health is essential for overall well-being.

## Daily Dental Habits

### Brush Twice Daily

Use fluoride toothpaste.

### Floss Regularly

Remove plaque between teeth.

### Visit Your Dentist

Schedule checkups every six months.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I visit a dentist?

Every six months.

### Is flossing important?

Yes, it removes plaque from hard-to-reach areas.
